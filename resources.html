Implicit bias denotes the unconscious attitudes or stereotypes that affect our choices and interactions, frequently operating without our knowledge. The Implicit Bias Test (IBT) serves to help individuals identify these concealed biases, fostering self-examination and awareness. Intersectionality, a concept put forth by Kimberlé Crenshaw, underscores how intersecting identities—like race, gender, and class—result in intricate patterns of privilege and discrimination.
